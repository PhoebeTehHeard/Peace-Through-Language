What This Platform Can Do for You

We allow you to:

    Paste or upload text (political speeches, social media posts, statements, essays, etc.)

    Run it through our AI agent connected to:

      i.our harm detection corpus

      ii.our linguistic classifier

      iii.our GSR‑based emotional impact model (visualisation)

    Receive suggestions for:

      i.rephrasing harmful segments

      ii.constructive alternatives

      iii.peace‑oriented language

      iv.sentiment/emotion impact levels

    You can refer to these suggestions and use them to improve your text.
